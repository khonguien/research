Solution for Magnetometer Board

I have been reviewing the author's previous research regarding alternative boards for the sensor. Luckily, I found that the magnetometer board used in both the previous studies and our current sensor is identical. Furthermore, the BOM, Gerber files, NC Drill files, and Pick & Place files are all open-source.

I suggest contacting a local PCB manufacturer to fabricate the magnetometer board. This would significantly reduce lead times, as international shipping is currently our primary bottleneck.

The information below details the required components and the specifications for requesting a quote from the supplier.

Important Note: Please be aware that the core magnetometer chip required for this board, the MLX90393, is currently experiencing a severe global supply shortage (very low-stock)

Specifications for Requesting Quotes form Manufacturers

  1. Navigate to the supplier's website and select PCB Instant Quote option.
  2. The rigid board is required for the magnetometer circuit. At the website of the PCB manufacturer, upload the zip file contains both "Gerber", "NC Drill" and "Pick Place" files. After that, using the athor recommended settings below for the board.
    • Board type: Single Pieces
    • Different Design in Panel: 1
    • Size: (auto-populated) 20x29mm
    • Quantity: 5 is comfortable as the supplier often require a quote at least 5 units
    • Layers: 2 Layers
    • Material: FR-4
    • FR4-TG: TG130-140
    • Thickness: 0.6
    • Min Track/Spacing: 6/6mil
    • Min Hole Size: 0.3mm
    • Solder Mask: Green
    • Silkscreen: White
    • Edge Connector: No
    • Surface Finish: HASL with Lead or Immersion gold (ENIG)
    • Via Process: Tenting Vias
    • Finished Copper: 1oz Cu
    • Additional options: None
  3. Assembly service
    • Turnkey
    • Board type: single pieces
    • Quantity: 2 to 3 units as presented with 1 unit for backup
    • Pay attention: No sensitive parts
    • Number of unique parts: 6
    • Number of SMD parts: 6
    • Number of BGA/QFP parts: 0
    • Number of through-hole parts: 0
